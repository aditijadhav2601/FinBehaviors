About the Project

FinBehaviors evaluates how instantaneous transaction pathways systematically degrade natural spending reflection milestones across differing global markets.

As payments transition into completely frictionless biometric scans, one-click checkout loops, and background auto-renewals, our psychological barriers to purchasing disintegrate.
